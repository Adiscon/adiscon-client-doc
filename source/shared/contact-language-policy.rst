Language policy for customer requests
=====================================

This policy applies to both sales and technical support requests.

Preferred languages
-------------------

English and German are equally well supported.

Other languages
---------------

Other languages are accepted and may be processed with AI assistance.

Japanese support context
------------------------

Japanese users can contact their reseller for Japanese-language assistance.
Depending on reseller capabilities, this can include technical support.

For products with Japanese documentation builds (for example WinSyslog-J),
Japanese-language help files are available.

Contact channels
----------------

For sales and support requests, use the
`Customer Service System <https://ticket.adiscon.com>`_ as the primary channel.
Email is also accepted and creates tickets.
